About Me

A timeline of the places I've lived.

Seoul

I was born in Seoul, where my story begins.

Xinjiang

I spent two years of my early childhood in Xinjiang, which became one of the first places that shaped my sense of environment and memory.

Almaty

Almaty is where I grew up and where I graduated from high school. It is the place that shaped much of who I am.

Abu Dhabi

I have spent the last three years in Abu Dhabi, where I grew academically and developed a clearer sense of the work I want to do.
